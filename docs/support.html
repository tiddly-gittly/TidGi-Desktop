Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Does Singlebox support Amazon Music, Amazon Prime Video, BBC iPlayer, Hulu, Netflix and Spotify?
No, it doesn't. We would love to support these apps. But they rely on a technology call WidevineDRM which is not available in Singlebox because of technical and legal constraints [1] [2].
